How to read the stages

  • Transport / pre-analytical delay: time from collection until the sample effectively enters the routine laboratory workflow.
  • Waiting for intake / registration: queue before sample intake begins.
  • Waiting for laboratory processing: queue before the sample reaches the main analytical workflow.
  • Waiting for authorisation: queue before result release/authorisation.
